The thesis set out to investigate how peri-urbanization processes influenced the livelihoods of peri-urban indigenes, and to what extent haveland governance systems supported their livelihoods strategies through mediating these processes. This chapter, by revisiting the main research questions, summarizes the findings and offerings of this thesis. Based on the research findings, it provides some thoughts and recommendations on livelihood intervention strategies in peri-urban regions. These recommendations are not set as blueprint or magic wand which can fix the challenges in all contexts.
